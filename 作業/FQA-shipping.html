SHIPPING

When will my order be shipped?

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1–2 business days after purchase.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Currently, we do not offer same-day delivery. All orders are processed within 1–2 business days and shipped based on the selected delivery method.

What if my order arrives damaged or incomplete?

If your order arrives damaged or items are missing, please contact us at support@fitwell.com within 48 hours of delivery.
