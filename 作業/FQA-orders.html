FQA

Everything you need to know before your first bite.

ORDERS

How do I know if my order was successful?

You will receive a confirmation email once your order is placed.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Yes, you can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it.

I didn’t get an order confirmation. What should I do?

Please check your spam or junk folder to make sure the confirmation email didn’t end up there.

Can I place an order without creating an account?

Yes, you can place an order as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track your orders, save your shipping information, and enjoy a faster checkout experience in the future.

Where can I view my order history?

You can view your order history by logging into your Fitwell account and going to the “My Orders” section. If you checked out as a guest, please refer to the order confirmation email you received.

I entered the wrong shipping address. Can I fix it?

If you entered the wrong shipping address, please contact us immediately at support@fitwell.com. If your order hasn’t been processed yet, we’ll do our best to update the address. However, once the order has shipped, we’re unable to make any changes.

Do you offer gift orders?

Yes, we offer gift orders! During checkout, you can select the “This is a gift” option and add a personalized message.

PAYMENT

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.

Can I get a receipt or invoice?

Yes! A digital receipt is automatically sent to your email after purchase.

My payment didn’t go through. What should I do?

If your payment didn’t go through, please double-check your card details, available balance, or try a different payment method.

Can I pay with cash on delivery?

Currently, we do not offer cash on delivery.

SHIPPING

When will my order be shipped?

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1–2 business days after purchase.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Currently, we do not offer same-day delivery. All orders are processed within 1–2 business days and shipped based on the selected delivery method.

What if my order arrives damaged or incomplete?

If your order arrives damaged or items are missing, please contact us at support@fitwell.com within 48 hours of delivery.
